California Nut Crimes

source: wikipedia article

California nut crimes refers to the organised theft of nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews, and pecans) in California.1,2 Reported cases of nut theft go as far back as 2006 with the worth of stolen nuts being millions of dollars.3 The thefts are sophisticated in nature involving identity theft and a knowledge of computer security and logistics.4
